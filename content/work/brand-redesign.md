# Project Overview

Lumina AI had outgrown their startup roots and needed a mature, trustworthy brand identity that still felt cutting-edge. Their primary goal was to appeal to enterprise clients while maintaining their technical credibility.

## Process

The visual exploration focused on themes of light, data flow, and clarity. We moved away from the generic "tech blue" and embraced a stark, high-contrast black and white palette with subtle gradients.

- **Logo Design**: A minimalist monogram that doubles as a data visualization motif.
- **Web Experience**: Built a high-performance marketing site using Next.js and Framer Motion, featuring interactive 3D elements.
- **Collateral**: Designed business cards, pitch decks, and digital assets.

## Outcome

The rebrand successfully positioned Lumina AI as an industry leader, contributing to a successful Series B funding round shortly after launch.
